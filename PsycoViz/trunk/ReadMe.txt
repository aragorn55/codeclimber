-------------
  PsycoViz
-------------

PsycoViz is a data visualization grid that shows all the books and seminars of psychologists
along a timeline and with also shows the connections among the books and the concepts treated
in the books and seminars.

Please refer to the file Psycoviz.doc for more detailed information about the application.
